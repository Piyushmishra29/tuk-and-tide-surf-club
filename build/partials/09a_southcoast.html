Sense of Place · The South Coast

Where We Ride

Tuk & Tide is Weligama to the bone — red-clay roads, a salt haze on the bay, and a whole island of characters between here and the next break. Five hand-carved south-coast marks, each one a little postcard of Sri Lanka to scatter on tickets, totes, tags and tuk-tuk panels.

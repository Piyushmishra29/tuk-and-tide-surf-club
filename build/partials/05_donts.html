Logo Don'ts

The mark is hand-carved and sun-faded — treat it that way. Six quick ways to wreck it. Don't.

✗
Don't stretch it.
Never squash or stretch the mark. Keep it locked to its original proportions.
✗
Don't tilt it.
No rotating, angling or jaunty tilts. The tuk rolls level, always.
✗
Don't go off-brand.
No clashing backgrounds or recoloured ink. Stick to the five heritage tones.
✗
Don't add effects.
No glows, drop-shadows, outlines or gradients. The screenprint is flat by design.
✗

Don't crowd it.
Give the mark room to breathe. Keep type and edges clear of its safe space.
✗
Don't shrink it small.
Below the minimum size the carving turns to mud. Hold the floor and the detail.

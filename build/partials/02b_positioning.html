No. 02 · The Stance
Positioning Statement
The easygoing way to surf
Sri Lanka's south coast —
board under your arm, tuk-tuk at the curb.
One club. Two wheels. Endless sets.
What we are
A local surf & tuk-tuk club in Weligama — rentals, lessons and coast-line tours, run by people who actually live the lineup.
Who it's for
First-timers and frequent flyers alike — anyone who wants the south coast made simple, salty and sincere.
What we stand for
01
Salt-cured craft
Boards waxed, tuks tuned, routes known by heart. Nothing slapdash leaves the shed.
02
Local & proud
Born on Weligama sand. Our crew, our reefs, our roads — shared the way a neighbour would.
03
Easy stoke
No ego, no rush. Good waves should feel like a long, lazy weekend.
04
Ride anywhere
Curb to coast on three wheels. If there's a wave worth chasing, we'll get you there.
05
Good company
You arrive a guest and leave a regular. The session is half the story.

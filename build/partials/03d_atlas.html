Weligama · South Coast
A field guide to home waters

The South
Coast

our island, hand-painted
Twenty kilometres of warm reef, point and beach-break, strung between palm and paddy along the deep south. Pick a wave, flag a three-wheeler, and chase the swell.
1Weligama Bay
Soft, forgiving sand-bottom rollers — where almost everyone stands up for the first time. Our home water.
2Mirissa
Mornings for blue whales offshore; evenings for the longest, reddest sunsets on the coast.
3Midigama
The Lazy Left and Ram’s Right — shallow reef, quick walls, for when you’re ready to graduate.
4Ahangama
Long, peeling points that keep going well past breakfast. Patience and a good paddle.
Boards on the roof-rack, salt in your hair — the tuk-tuk is simply how you get from one break to the next.

The Club

We're Tuk & Tide Club.

Born on the warm sand of Weligama, we're the crew that hands you a board for the waves and a tuk-tuk for the road — then gets out of your way so you can have a good time.

We're locals at heart, the kind who know which break is firing, where the best roti stop hides, and how to dodge the afternoon traffic. No stiff counters, no fine print, no stress. Just sand, surf and three wheels — and a friendly face who'll sort the rest. Come for the swell, stay for the ride. We'll make your trip easy, salty and a whole lot of fun.

Clear Space

Keep breathing room equal to the height of the wordmark's & on all sides of any lockup. Nothing — type, edges or other marks — enters that zone.

Minimum Size

Badge never smaller than 16 mm wide; wordmark never below 30 mm wide. Stamp use stands in for a single-ink reproduction where colour printing isn't possible.

Logo Don'ts

The badge works hard for us — so treat it kindly. Keep its shape, its colours and its breathing room intact. Here's what to never do.

✗
Don't stretch or distort the badge.
✗
Don't rotate or tilt it off level.
✗
Don't drop it on clashing colours.
✗
RENT NOW!
Don't crowd it — give it space.
✗
Don't add outlines, glows or effects.
✗
Don't shrink below the minimum size.

How We Sound

Voice & Tone

Laid-back local soul with a let's-go grin. We sound salty, friendly and a touch cheeky — like a mate handing you a board, never a counter reading you the terms. Warm over slick, easy over pushy, always.

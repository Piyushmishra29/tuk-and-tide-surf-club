We're Tuk & Tide

Born in Weligama Bay, we're a small crew of local surfers and tuk-tuk drivers who never could tell the two apart. Board under your arm, three wheels to the break — that's the whole idea.

Easygoing and salt-cured, we run on early swells and good company. Come for a lesson, grab a board, or load up for a coast-long surf safari. However you ride, you ride with us.

— the Tuk & Tide crew
